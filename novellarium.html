Problem statement

In today's crowded online book market, most platforms prioritize profit over the reader's experience. Novellarium bucks this trend by offering a dedicated space for book enthusiasts to buy, sell, and connect. I led the entire design process, from initial research and user testing to the final UX/UI, ensuring a platform that truly serves the needs of book lovers.

User-Flow

To gain a comprehensive understanding of my website's structure and ensure a seamless user experience, I built a user flow in Figma Jam. This visual representation helped me identify potential navigation issues, optimize the user journey, and prioritize development efforts based on user needs.

Wireframes

In the initial stages of my app design, I conducted thorough research into existing successful applications to identify and understand the most intuitive and user-friendly interaction patterns. This research served as a valuable foundation for my design process.

I commenced the design process with rapid low-fidelity sketches on paper, allowing for quick exploration of various ideas and layouts. These initial sketches helped me to quickly visualize and iterate on core concepts and user flows.

Subsequently, I transitioned to mid-fidelity wireframes utilizing Figma. This stage involved creating more detailed and refined representations of the user interface, including key elements such as screens, navigation, and interactive components. The use of Figma enabled me to refine the design iteratively.

User Testing

To ensure my screen layouts met users' wants and needs, I initiated user testing. Using the program Maze, I gathered valuable feedback that was instrumental in refining my designs. This iterative process of user testing and design refinement ensured that the final product was user-centered and met the needs of the target audience.

Conclusion

By focusing on user needs, we've created a platform that enhances the book-buying experience and fosters a sense of community. Challenges like implementing advanced filtering and integrating feedback have provided valuable lessons for future improvements. We are committed to refining our app, expanding features, and ensuring it remains a beloved destination for book enthusiasts.

Next Iteration

  • A dedicated returns page.
  • A book trading features.
